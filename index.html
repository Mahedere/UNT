About Us

UNT Cable Installation PLC is a private company registered with the Ethiopian Communication Authority (License No. ECA/AA/13400/2017) to work in the telecommunications industry in Ethiopia. We are a competitive private company that provides fast telecommunication services.

Our Mission

To provide quality network service to customers in rural and urban areas of our country, as well as reliable, standardized, and consistent network deployment.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted provider of standard fiber optics services to customers and to create quality network capacity for smart city construction.

Our Values

Reliability, Customer Satisfaction, Ethics and Transparency, Professionalism and Innovation.
